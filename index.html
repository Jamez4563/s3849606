Overview

This project will be an assistive technology app, aimed towards users who have a disability. The aim is for the app to first be compatible on both google play and app store. The pp will have multiple functions such as being able to zoom in far more than the regular phone camera, through the use of AI it will help make the picture more clear and through coding it will reduce motion on a digital side, in other words increase a digital optical image stabilisation. The app shall also consist of being able to scan text and reformat to an alternative format accessible by low vision users, it shall also be able to convert the text to speech for users who have no sense of hearing.

Motivation

As a vision impaired user myself, I find it hard to read text in different fonts and sizes, I often encounter situations where I just cannot see the sign or light so will resort to using my phone. Many times, the phone camera is not enough as it is not built for accessibility purposes. When reading books or documentation, it is very hard to read anything, it takes me a lot less time to just listen to the audio of the document at a higher rate of speed, which is why I think that many users regardless if deaf, blind or any other disability, will find this program extremely useful. I do have something similar, but it just isn’t as convenient as all purposes are split into different apps, so I need to keep switching for the right function.

Description

The app would have several capabilities, such as changing the theme of the text after scanning, such as invert colours, Yellow on Black, White on Black, etc. It would have an option to speed up the rate of speech, for those users who rely on audio formats, the ability to change the voice style from different audio files. Have an inbuilt camera function using the phones back cameras, have a built in AI to help stabilise and help make the picture clearer, through the technology the app would be using multiple functions to provide a clear result. Such as, if taking a photo of very small text, the app would use its scan, camera, digital optical image stabilisation functionalities to combine all three data into one set of data which would be a result picture. The app would have the ability to pair up hearing aids and customise noise levels and different frequencies to ensure that the user is comfortable while using the program. The app would also have an inbuilt ‘night mode’ function built into the camera, what this will do is act as a brightener for the picture if taking photos late at night or in a dark environment. As far as brightening the image, it cannot go as far due to a physical capability of the lens, however the app would be able to use the previous data set from all three functions and add an additional function which would shift the automatic focus of the camera and turn it off. Then import various tools to increase the brightness of the photo after storing the photo in the phone’s storage. Touching back to the alternative formats, the app would have present formats which are easier to read, such as bolded, or less points and more curves. This would also include the ability to change font colour, font size as well as font thickness manually. If editing the scanned document, it would have a present curser for typing which can be adjusted through a horizontal slider to decrease and increase the thickness of the curser for visibility.

Tools and Technology

The app is intended to be built for android and iOS devices, however taking into all the functions that will be running simultaneously, it would require a phone with a recent processor as it would require a bit of RAM and processing power from the processor. The app is planned to eb built in a mobile app creator such as MIT App Inventor and is planned to eb coded completely out of JavaSCript or Python.
